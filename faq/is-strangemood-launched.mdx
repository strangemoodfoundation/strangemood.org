---
question: "Is Strangemood launched?"
category: "basics"
---

Yes. The protocol is alive, and there's a program on the Mainnet of Solana. However, the Strangemood Foundation's client applications are invite only at the moment. You're welcome to build your own client app and start right now, or you can apply to [try out the beta by joining the Strangemood Foundation.](https://www.strangemood.org/welcome)
